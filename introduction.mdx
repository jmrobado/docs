What is Wooice?

Wooice is an AI Agency that publishes **pre-built** and **fully functional** AI Agents so you can adapt them to your website in a simple way without any technical knowledge.

## Onboarding

[Sign up](https://wooice.com/join) on our website and **choose** the pre-built AI Agent you want to use from our library.

In 24 hours, you will receive an email with the **password** to your dashboard where your chosen AI Agent will be ready to be customized.
